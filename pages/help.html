Frequently Asked Questions
Vehicle Gate Access Issue

If the campus gate doesn't open for your registered vehicle, ensure your plate is entered correctly and the permit is still active. Email support if the issue persists.

Can't Sign In

Make sure you are using your official university email (e.g. name@gu.edu.eg). If you've forgotten your password, use the "Forgot password?" link on the login page.

Course or GPA Questions

For questions about course enrollment, grades, or GPA calculations, try the AI Assistant first. For official academic disputes, contact support via email.

AI Assistant Not Responding

If the AI Assistant is unavailable or giving incorrect information, please refresh the page. For urgent academic matters, contact support directly.

Report a Bug or Issue

Found something broken on the portal? Send a detailed description including which page and what happened to our support email and we'll address it promptly.

Account & Privacy

For account access issues, password resets, or privacy-related inquiries — contact support at the email above. We respond within 1 business day.
